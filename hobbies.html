Reading

My main hobby is reading. This is what i spent most of my free time doing. I read everyday after college and for a while before bed each night.

My favourite book series is Throne of Glass by Sarah J. Maas. It is an 8 book fantasy series which follows the journey an 18 year old assassin through a world filled with secrets, magic, and power struggles. Along the way she uncovers the truth about the world and ends in an epic confrontation. I even used the popular quote as my yearbook quote in secondary school.

"To whatever end"

Watching Movies
