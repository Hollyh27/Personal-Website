The Last of Us


About the game

The game is set in a post-apocalyptic world where a fungal infection has transformed most of humanity into aggressive, zombie-like creatures known as the Infected.

Have you played The Last of Us before?

Plot Summary

The story follows Joel, a hardened survivor, and Ellie, a young teenager, as they journey across the United States. Joel is tasked with escorting Ellie to a group called the Fireflies, who believe Ellie may hold the key to creating a cure for the infection. Along the way, they face numerous challenges, including hostile humans and the Infected, while forming a deep bond with each other.
